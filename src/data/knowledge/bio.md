# About Kyle

I'm a full-time software developer at Robins Financial Credit Union, where I've worked for 6 years — 4.5 of those in the programming department. KYGR Solutions is what I do on the side, in my free time, for people whose ideas I want to help bring to life.

I've worked with a few local businesses so far:
- **Lexis Fresh Slate Cleanings** — a local cleaning company. I built the full front-end site plus admin, client, and employee portals.
- **Mid-State Baptist Association** — revitalized their website to bring their online presence back to life.
- **Mise-AI** — a personal recipe-generation app I liked so much I decided to market it.
- **A local realtor (in progress)** — an AI-powered phone assistant that answers her initial client calls, runs basic qualification, and books meetings on her schedule, so she can stay focused on the clients she's already with.

The thing I love most about this work is building systems that genuinely make people's lives easier. The point isn't to help people be lazy — it's to take the friction out of running their business so they have time for the things software can't do, like building real relationships with their clients and being present with their family.

I started KYGR Solutions because I love full-stack work and I love the moment when someone's idea — the thing they've been carrying around in their head — finally exists and starts working for them.

The reason I care about easing people's load isn't accidental. I follow Christ, and that conviction is what drives how I treat clients, how I price my work, and what I'm willing to take on. I'd rather you have time for your family and your customers than have the fanciest software.
