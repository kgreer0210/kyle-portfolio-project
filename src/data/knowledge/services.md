# What I Do

KYGR Solutions is a one-person side practice. The work I take on falls into five buckets, and most projects are some combination of these:

## 1. Custom web apps & portals
Full-stack builds where a business needs more than a website — they need a working tool. Admin dashboards, client portals, employee portals, custom workflows. Example: the full front-end + admin/client/employee portal I built for Lexis Fresh Slate Cleanings.

## 2. Marketing & business websites
Modern, fast, easy-to-update sites for small businesses, ministries, and local organizations. Example: the revitalized site for Mid-State Baptist Association.

## 3. AI assistants & automations
Chat assistants, voice/phone agents that field initial calls, AI-powered lead qualification, and automations that handle the repeatable work so a small team doesn't have to. Example: the AI phone assistant currently in progress for a local realtor that answers her initial calls, handles qualification, and books meetings on her schedule.

## 4. Integrations & internal tooling
Stitching existing tools (CRMs, calendars, payments, email, scheduling) into a single workflow that just works for the client — instead of forcing them to bounce between five tabs to run their business.

## 5. Mobile apps
Mobile apps for iOS and Android — whether you need a customer-facing app, an internal tool for your team, or a companion to a web platform we build together.

## What I'm honest about turning down

- **Enterprise-scale or 24/7-mission-critical systems.** I'm a side practice with a full-time day job. I'm not the right fit for systems that need on-call support or formal SLAs.
- **Projects that conflict with my faith or values** (gambling, adult content, anything dishonest, etc.). I follow Christ, and I'd rather refer a project elsewhere than take on work I'm not at peace building.

## When I'm not sure

If something doesn't fit cleanly into the buckets above, the right move is usually a 30-minute call — most of the work I enjoy starts as a conversation, not a checkbox.
